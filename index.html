Somtavin Café

Somtavin café is not new to my knowledge as some of my friends visited this place few months before my daughter and I did. It is recommended to travel by a private vehicle towards this café as this is located 30 minutes from the City of Pattaya. Parking area are situated right in front of Somtavin café. Right in front of the Entrance area they have a small place that displaces framed photos of customers.
